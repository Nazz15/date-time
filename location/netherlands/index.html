Frequently Asked Questions

What timezone is Netherlands in?

Netherlands uses UTC+01:00.

Does Netherlands observe Daylight Saving Time?

The Netherlands observes Daylight Saving Time across the EU from late March to late October.

What is the time difference between Netherlands and London or New York?

Amsterdam is 1 hour ahead of London (GMT/BST) and 6 hours ahead of New York (EST/EDT). These gaps shift slightly across the year as other regions observe their own Daylight Saving Time changes.

What currency does Netherlands use?

Netherlands uses the Euro (EUR, symbol €). Cards are widely accepted in major cities; carry some cash for smaller vendors.

What is the best time to visit Netherlands?

April to May for tulip season or June to August for warm weather and long daylight hours. Conditions can vary significantly between regions, so check the specific city you're visiting for more precise guidance.
