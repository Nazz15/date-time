Frequently Asked Questions

What timezone is Germany in?

Germany uses UTC+01:00.

Does Germany observe Daylight Saving Time?

Germany observes Daylight Saving Time across the EU from late March to late October.

What is the time difference between Germany and London or New York?

Berlin is 1 hour ahead of London (GMT/BST) and 6 hours ahead of New York (EST/EDT). These gaps shift slightly across the year as other regions observe their own Daylight Saving Time changes.

What currency does Germany use?

Germany uses the Euro (EUR, symbol €). Cards are widely accepted in major cities; carry some cash for smaller vendors.

What is the best time to visit Germany?

May to September for warm weather, or late November to December for Christmas markets. Conditions can vary significantly between regions, so check the specific city you're visiting for more precise guidance.
