Frequently Asked Questions

What timezone is Germany in?

Germany uses UTC+01:00. Based on its capital, Berlin, this is 1 hour ahead of London (GMT/BST) and 6 hours ahead of New York (EST/EDT).

Does Germany observe Daylight Saving Time?

Germany observes Daylight Saving Time across the EU from late March to late October. This affects how far ahead or behind Germany is compared to other countries at different times of year.

What currency does Germany use?

Germany uses the Euro (EUR, symbol €). Carry local currency for smaller vendors, though cards are widely accepted in major cities.

What language is spoken in Germany?

The official language of Germany is German. Visitors relying on English should check ahead, as prevalence varies between major cities and rural areas.

What is the best time to visit Germany?

May to September for warm weather, or late November to December for Christmas markets. Conditions can vary significantly between regions in Germany, so check the specific city you're visiting for more precise guidance.

What is Germany known for?

Germany is known for Christmas markets, Oktoberfest, the Black Forest, and historic cities like Berlin and Munich. It's located in Europe (Western Europe).
