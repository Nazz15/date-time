Frequently Asked Questions

What timezone is United Arab Emirates in?

United Arab Emirates uses UTC+04:00.

Does United Arab Emirates observe Daylight Saving Time?

The UAE does not observe Daylight Saving Time and keeps a fixed UTC+04:00 offset year-round.

What is the time difference between United Arab Emirates and London or New York?

Abu Dhabi is 3 hours ahead of London (GMT/BST) and 8 hours ahead of New York (EST/EDT). These gaps shift slightly across the year as other regions observe their own Daylight Saving Time changes.

What currency does United Arab Emirates use?

United Arab Emirates uses the UAE Dirham (AED, symbol د.إ). Cards are widely accepted in major cities; carry some cash for smaller vendors.

What is the best time to visit United Arab Emirates?

November through March when daytime temperatures are far more comfortable for outdoor sightseeing. Conditions can vary significantly between regions, so check the specific city you're visiting for more precise guidance.
