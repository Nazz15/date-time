Frequently Asked Questions

What timezone is United Arab Emirates in?

United Arab Emirates uses UTC+04:00. Based on its capital, Abu Dhabi, this is 3 hours ahead of London (GMT/BST) and 8 hours ahead of New York (EST/EDT).

Does United Arab Emirates observe Daylight Saving Time?

The UAE does not observe Daylight Saving Time and keeps a fixed UTC+04:00 offset year-round. This affects how far ahead or behind United Arab Emirates is compared to other countries at different times of year.

What currency does United Arab Emirates use?

United Arab Emirates uses the UAE Dirham (AED, symbol د.إ). Carry local currency for smaller vendors, though cards are widely accepted in major cities.

What language is spoken in United Arab Emirates?

The official language of United Arab Emirates is Arabic. Visitors relying on English should check ahead, as prevalence varies between major cities and rural areas.

What is the best time to visit United Arab Emirates?

November through March when daytime temperatures are far more comfortable for outdoor sightseeing. Conditions can vary significantly between regions in United Arab Emirates, so check the specific city you're visiting for more precise guidance.
