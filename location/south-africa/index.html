Frequently Asked Questions

What timezone is South Africa in?

South Africa uses UTC+02:00.

Does South Africa observe Daylight Saving Time?

South Africa does not observe Daylight Saving Time and keeps a fixed UTC+02:00 offset year-round.

What is the time difference between South Africa and London or New York?

Pretoria is 1 hour ahead of London (GMT/BST) and 6 hours ahead of New York (EST/EDT). These gaps shift slightly across the year as other regions observe their own Daylight Saving Time changes.

What currency does South Africa use?

South Africa uses the South African Rand (ZAR, symbol R). Cards are widely accepted in major cities; carry some cash for smaller vendors.

What is the best time to visit South Africa?

May to September for dry winter weather, which is also peak season for game viewing in national parks. Conditions can vary significantly between regions, so check the specific city you're visiting for more precise guidance.
