Frequently Asked Questions

What timezone is Singapore in?

Singapore uses UTC+08:00. Based on its capital, Singapore, this is 7 hours ahead of London (GMT/BST) and 12 hours ahead of New York (EST/EDT).

Does Singapore observe Daylight Saving Time?

Singapore does not observe Daylight Saving Time and keeps a fixed UTC+08:00 offset year-round. This affects how far ahead or behind Singapore is compared to other countries at different times of year.

What currency does Singapore use?

Singapore uses the Singapore Dollar (SGD, symbol $). Carry local currency for smaller vendors, though cards are widely accepted in major cities.

What language is spoken in Singapore?

The official language of Singapore is English/Mandarin/Malay. Visitors relying on English should check ahead, as prevalence varies between major cities and rural areas.
