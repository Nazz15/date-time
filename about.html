Our Mission

Coordinating across time zones shouldn't require five browser tabs and a headache. TimezoneBudy brings together a world clock, timezone converter, meeting planner, weather, and sun & moon data into one fast, free toolkit — built for remote teams, travelers, and anyone juggling time across borders.

We built TimezoneBudy because existing tools felt cluttered, slow, or locked behind paywalls for basic features. Our goal is simple: give people accurate, instant time information with zero friction.

Fast by Default

No heavy frameworks, no bloated downloads. Pages load instantly and calculations happen in your browser.

Privacy Respecting

We don't track your location or sell your data. Your pinned cities are stored only in your own browser.

Free to Use

Every tool on TimezoneBudy is free, with no feature paywalls or forced sign-ups.

What We Offer

  • World Clock — live local time for 130+ cities, in table, compact, digital, or analog view
  • Timezone Converter — convert any time between cities instantly, plus a visual meeting planner
  • Sun & Moon — sunrise, sunset, moon phase, and illumination for any location
  • Weather — current conditions and 14-day forecasts powered by Open-Meteo
  • Location Directory — browse 20 countries and their major cities with live time and key facts
